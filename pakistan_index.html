    • Government Type: Federal Parliamentary Republic
    • President (2024): Asif Ali Zardari
    • Prime Minister (2024): Shehbaz Sharif
    • Legislature: Bicameral — National Assembly and Senate
    • Judiciary: Supreme Court, High Courts in provinces
    • Armed Forces: Army, Navy, Air Force — Pakistan is a nuclear power
  5. Economy
    • GDP (Nominal, 2024): ~$375 Billion USD
    • Major Industries: Textiles, Agriculture, Construction, Pharmaceuticals
    • Main Exports: Textiles, Rice, Leather, Sports Goods, Surgical Instruments
    • Main Imports: Petroleum, Machinery, Vehicles, Chemicals
    • Agriculture: Wheat, Cotton, Sugarcane, Rice, Fruits
    • Currency Code: PKR
    • Stock Exchange: Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)
  6. Culture and Society
    • Major Religion: Islam (~96%)
    • Minorities: Christians, Hindus, Sikhs, Ahmadis
    • National Dress: Shalwar Kameez
    • Popular Foods: Biryani, Nihari, Karahi, Haleem, Roti
    • National Sports: Hockey (official), Cricket (most popular)
    • Other Sports: Football, Kabaddi, Squash, Polo
    • Famous Cultural Events: Basant, Independence Day (Aug 14), Eid Festivals

  8. History Highlights
    • 1947: Independence from British India (August 14)
    • 1948: Founder Muhammad Ali Jinnah dies
    • 1956: Pakistan becomes an Islamic Republic
    • 1971: East Pakistan becomes Bangladesh
    • 1998: Nuclear tests conducted in Chagai
    • 2001–2022: War on terror, economic crises, political instability
